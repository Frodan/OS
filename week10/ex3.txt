After removing execute permission:
-rw-r--r-- (644)
After all permissions to owner and others(not group):
-rwx---rwx (707)
After group permissions equal to user permissions:
-rwxrwxrwx (777)
660 means -rw-rw---- (read\write for owner and group)
775 means -rwxrwxr-x (all perms for owner and group, others can only read\execute)
777 means -rwxrwxrwx (all perms for all)
